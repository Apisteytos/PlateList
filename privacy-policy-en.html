Privacy Policy for PlateList App

Last updated: 25/09/2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the PlateList application ("we", "our app") collects, uses, and protects your information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Data You Enter

  • Food items you consume
  • Meal dates and times
  • Food and drink quantities
  • Physical activity data (gym visits)

2.2 Technical Data

  • Device information for app optimization
  • Usage data through Google AdMob

3. How We Use Your Data

3.1 Core Functions

  • Store and display your food entries
  • Create reports and statistics
  • Export your data to PDF format

3.2 App Improvement

  • Usage analysis for feature optimization
  • Advertisements through Google AdMob

4. Data Storage

4.1 Local Storage

  • All personal food data is stored exclusively on your device
  • No data is sent to external servers
  • You have complete control over your data

4.2 Security

  • We use industry-standard security measures
  • Data is protected by Android's security framework

5. Data Sharing

5.1 Third Parties

We DO NOT sell, rent, or share your personal data. Only exception: Google AdMob for advertisement display.

5.2 Google AdMob

  • We use Google AdMob for advertisements
  • AdMob may collect anonymous data for personalized ads
  • You can manage ad preferences in Android settings

6. Your Rights

6.1 Access and Deletion

  • You have full access to your data through the app
  • You can delete any entry at any time
  • You can uninstall the app for complete data removal

6.2 Data Portability

  • You can export your data to PDF through the app
  • Data is exported to your device

7. Children

Our app is not directed at children under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

8. App Permissions

8.1 Permissions We Use

  • Internet/Network Access: For displaying AdMob advertisements
  • Storage Access (Android 9 and earlier): For PDF file export

8.2 Why We Need These Permissions

  • We do not access your personal files
  • Internet access is used only for advertisements

9. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy. We will notify you of significant changes through app updates.

10. Important Health Notice

PlateList app is a food tracking tool and DOES NOT provide medical advice. For health and nutrition matters, always consult a healthcare professional or nutritionist.
